MAGSUMBOL FAMILY

Our family is different, but at the same time,
we are the same. My brother loves football
while I enjoy basketball. My brother is a
singer while I am a dancer. My mother is
a businesswoman while my father is an
office worker. We all love to go on vacations,
watch movies, and go to Church. So I can
say we are different, but at the same time,
the same.
